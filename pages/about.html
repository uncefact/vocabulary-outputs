About

The UN/CEFACT Sustainability Web Vocabulary is a linked data representation of the International Trade Centre (ITC) harmonised sustainability criteria. It has been developed from an analysis of over 300 sustainability standards in use around the world and represents a harmonised language for Environmental and Social Governance (ESDG) sustainability criteria.
